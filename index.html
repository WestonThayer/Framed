Framed

Rapidly prototype with Framer. Interact with your designs on any Windows device.

Phones

  • Fullscreen - experience your design like a native app
  • Sync - connect and stay in sync with Framer
  • Refresh - just press the Camera button

PC & Tablet

  • Windowed - runs in a normal desktop window that can be position, resized
  • Full-bleed - hide the titlebar and create immersive experiences
  • Pen - bring the precision of a pen into your UX

Hololens (untested)

  • In the world - pin the window to real-world surfaces
  • Air tap - works like standard mouse input
  • VR? - might work :)

Xbox (coming soon)

  • Design for TV - be cinematic in the living room
  • Controllers - Use an Xbox controller or remote and prototype the focus flow
